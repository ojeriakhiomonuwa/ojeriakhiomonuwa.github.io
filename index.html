Cloud & DevOps Engineer.

I am passionate about building scalable systems and simplifying how technology works behind the scenes. My journey into tech started with a simple curiosity: understanding how things work and how ideas become real, usable products. Over time, that curiosity grew into a focus on cloud infrastructure and DevOps, where I get to design, automate, and improve systems that power modern applications. In many of the projects I’ve worked on, I’ve encountered systems that needed better structure, scalability, or reliability. I approach these challenges by implementing cloud solutions, automating workflows, and optimizing deployment pipelines. The outcome is always the same goal: faster delivery, more stable systems, and less friction for teams and users. But beyond the code and infrastructure, I care deeply about people. I enjoy guiding young adults in the right direction, sharing what I’ve learned, and helping others make better decisions about their future. For me, growth isn’t just personal, it’s something to be shared. When I’m not working, you’ll find me watching or playing football, exploring graphic design, or simply enjoying moments that spark creativity and balance. I’m always open to building, learning, and collaborating on meaningful projects.
